## How It Works

### Step 1

Create your account and complete a short eligibility form.

### Step 2

Our partnered licensed clinics review your information and arrange a consultation with a specialist doctor.

### Step 3

If approved by the prescribing clinician, your prescription is processed through a licensed UK pharmacy.

### Step 4

Approved patients can manage repeat orders, consultations, and support through the platform.

---

## Who May Qualify?

Medical cannabis treatment may be considered for eligible patients experiencing conditions such as:

- Chronic pain
- Anxiety
- PTSD
- Insomnia
- ADHD
- Neurological conditions
- Other qualifying conditions reviewed by clinicians

All prescriptions are subject to independent clinician assessment and approval.

## Important Information

Up In Smoke is an independent educational and patient onboarding platform.

We do not prescribe, dispense, or supply medical cannabis products. All medical consultations, prescriptions, and dispensing are handled solely by licensed healthcare professionals and regulated UK partner clinics and pharmacies.

Eligibility does not guarantee prescription approval.
